Dive the Red Sea

The Sinai Peninsula, a desert region between the Red Sea and Mediterranean Sea, is home to some of the world's best dive sites. The Bedouin village of Dahab is situated on the central coast of the Peninsula, and attracts a large number of diving tourists every year.
